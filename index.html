Instructions

  1. Check the pulic resource "show players" to view a list of players
  2. Additionally you can search a single player by id
  3. Next add a desired player to your list with the add player button
  4. Under your saved players(on the right) you can target a specific player(comment when to draft)
  5. Finally at any time you can remove a player from your list with the delete button at any time
